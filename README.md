# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
HR Analytics dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, demographics, job roles, and performance using DAX and interactive visuals.

#Problem Statement
This dashboard is created for checking the attrition rate based on the departements and which department has most and high attrition rate.

#Features
.Various types of charts and KPI cards to visualize attrition and workforce metrics.
.Interactive slicers including button slicers for gender and list slicers for departments,allowing easy data filtering.
.Use of DAX measures to calculate key metrics like attrition rate,average tenure and more.
.Clear and intuitive design for quick insights.

#Insights
.The Human Resources department has the lowest attrition count,indicating higher employee retention.
.The Research and Development department experiences the highest attrition,signaling potential challenges in employee satisfaction or work environment.
.Employees who have worked for more than 5 years are less likely to leave,showing strong long-term retention.
.The attrition rate is highest among freshers and early-career employees.
